Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about working together

How long does a typical backend project take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity. A simple API backend typically takes 2-4 weeks, while complex systems with AI integration can take 2-6 months. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What's included in your backend development services?

My services include custom backend development, API design, database architecture, AI model integration, testing, deployment, and post-launch support. I also offer ongoing maintenance and technical guidance.

Do you work with clients remotely?

Yes! I work with clients worldwide. Through video calls, project management tools, and regular communication, I ensure smooth collaboration regardless of location. Time zone differences are managed effectively.

What technologies do you specialize in?

I specialize in Python backend technologies including Django, Flask, FastAPI, and various databases (MySQL, PostgreSQL). For AI/ML, I work with TensorFlow, scikit-learn, Hugging Face, and Azure OpenAI services.

How do you handle project revisions?

I include 2-3 rounds of revisions in most project packages. Additional revisions are handled at an hourly rate. I believe in getting it right, so I work closely with clients throughout the process to minimize major changes.
